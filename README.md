# Plant-Analysis

## Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive analysis of plant inventory data sourced from Kaggle. The project demonstrates data cleaning, transformation, and analysis using industry-standard tools to derive actionable insights from the plant sales dataset.

---

## 📊 Project Workflow

### 1. **Data Source**
- **Source**: Kaggle
- **Dataset**: Plant inventory and sales data including product information, pricing, and categorization

### 2. **Data Cleaning**
- **Tool**: Power Query Editor (Excel)
- **Process**: 
  - Data validation and removal of inconsistencies
  - Standardization of plant names, categories, and pricing
  - Handling missing values
  - Data transformation for analysis

### 3. **Analysis & Visualization**
- **Tool**: Excel
- **Output**: Interactive dashboard and comprehensive analysis report
- **Format**: PNG visualization available in Results folder

---

## 🔍 Key Findings & Analysis

### Pricing Analysis
- **Deepest Discount**: Jade Plant shows the highest price point (₹444.18) with substantial reduction to special price (₹232.97)
- **Consistent Discounting**: Most plants show 10-20% discount from original to special price
- **Price Strategy**: High discounts indicate potential "loss leaders" or stock clearance strategies

### Inventory Composition

#### Plant Size Distribution
- **Medium Plants**: 73% of inventory (majority size category)
- **Large Plants**: 27% of inventory
- **Small Plants**: 0% (market opportunity identified)

#### Plant Location Preferences
- **Indoor Plants**: 193 units (primary focus)
- **Outdoor Plants**: 124 units
- **Both (Indoor & Outdoor)**: 123 units

#### Plant Type Distribution
- **Shrubs**: 112 units (highest category)
- **Flowers**: 100 units (second highest)
- **Other varieties**: Diverse mix of plant types

### Brand Performance
- **Market Leader**: UGADU dominates with 177 units (44% market share)
- **Second**: Alaistore with 80 units
- **Third**: Cloud-farming with significantly fewer units
- **Market Concentration**: Highly dependent on single supplier - risk factor identified

---

## 💡 Key Recommendations

1. **Product Diversification**: Introduce Small plants to capture desk-sized plant market segment
2. **Supplier Diversification**: Reduce dependency on UGADU to mitigate supply chain risks
3. **Pricing Strategy Review**: Assess loss-leader strategy for high-discount items like Jade Plant
4. **Market Expansion**: Focus on Indoor plants as they show strongest market preference
